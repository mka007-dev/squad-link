Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Keep It Playable

  • Be respectful in posts, chat, DMs, squads, events, and voice rooms.
  • Use reports for real safety or moderation issues, not disagreements after a match.
  • Share accurate rank, region, platform, and availability details so matchmaking works.

Not Allowed

  • Harassment, hate, threats, bullying, stalking, or targeted abuse.
  • Scams, phishing links, account selling, cheats, exploits, or malware.
  • Doxxing, private personal information, or pressure to reveal private details.
  • Spam, bot behavior, fake reports, or repeated unwanted messages.

Moderation

Admins can review reports, clear unsafe content, and restrict access where needed. Serious issues should also be reported to the relevant game or platform service.
