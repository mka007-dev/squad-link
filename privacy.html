Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

LobbyRush is a gamer community app for finding squads, posting updates, joining events, chatting, and building player profiles.

Information We Collect

  • Account details such as email address and Firebase user ID.
  • Profile details you choose to add, including display name, platform, region, game preferences, rank, bio, and linked gamer handles.
  • Community content such as posts, chat messages, squads, events, reports, match actions, notifications, and private messages.
  • Basic technical data needed for security, App Check, analytics, and service reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide sign-in, profiles, matchmaking, posts, chat, events, reports, and notifications.
  • To protect the app from abuse, spam, unauthorized access, and unsafe content.
  • To send account, verification, welcome, and match-related emails when those services are enabled.

What Other Users Can See

Public profiles, posts, public chat, squads, events, joins, invite requests, and RSVPs may be visible to other LobbyRush users. Private messages, match actions, notifications, and admin logs are restricted by Firebase security rules.

Data Storage

LobbyRush uses Firebase Authentication, Cloud Firestore, App Check, and optional Cloud Functions. Data is stored and processed by Firebase/Google Cloud services.

Your Choices

You can update your profile at any time. To request account or data removal, contact the app owner from the GitHub repository or Firebase project owner account.
